About SmartLists

SmartLists is the CS290 Final Project by Jim Landers, Carter Roeser, Connor McLaughlin and Yash Sankanagouda.

SmartLists integrates with the Spotify API to pull your listening history and playlist data in order to generate graphs and visualizations. SmartLists never stores your credentials or personal information. Anonymized or user-shared data may be stored on SmartLists servers in order to publicly display generated content.
